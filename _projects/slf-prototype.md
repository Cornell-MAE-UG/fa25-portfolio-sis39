## Client Pitch

### Problem
Spotted Lanternflies contaminate grapes during harvesting, leading to rejected loads and economic loss.

### Solution
We designed an inline mechanical system using a rotating brush to remove SLF during conveyance.

---

<a id="functional-prototype"></a>
## Functional Prototype

### Purpose
To remove SLF from grape clusters using a rotating brush system.

### What was tested
- Brush rotation speed  
- Bristle coverage  
- Conveyor stability  
- SLF removal efficiency  

### Results
- Optimal speed ≈ 500 RPM  
- ~72% SLF removal  
- No grape damage observed  

### Outcome
The prototype successfully removes SLF but needs improvement in brush coverage and conveyor stability.
